Liberation Day Celebration

On 01.11.2025 at 9:00 a.m., both NSS Unit I & II jointly organized the Puducherry Liberation Day Celebration at Perunthalaivar Kamarajar Arts College. Dr. S. Kangavel, Principal, hoisted the national flag, and NSS volunteers made all the arrangements for the event. Refreshments were provided to all the volunteers after the celebration
